FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

My question has few, if any, instructions. What gives?

The instructions are often vague by design. It’s up to you to figure out what the puzzle is asking for based on the information given. You’ll get the hang of it!

What am I looking for?

Each question is different, but as a general rule, you should look for patterns, associations, and themes in the images or text you see in the puzzle. Often, you may uncover a clue or riddle, the answer to which is the solution.

In what format should my answers be?

The answer to every puzzle is a string. This includes single and multi-word answers. In some cases, the puzzle may accept several similar answers, but usually we’re looking for a very specific response.

Can I get hints?

It will be up to the discretion of the A-team to give hints but it will be rare. In the case in which a hint is deemed necessary, we will post it to the Facebook event so that everyone can see it.

There are only thirty minutes left and we’re in the hunt! What happened to the scoreboard?

The scoreboard will shut off with thirty minutes left in the competition. Focus on getting those puzzles answered and make sure you join us at the awards presentation to see if you won!
